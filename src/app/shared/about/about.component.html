About IEC

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center (IEC) was founded in 2019 within the Lebanese International University (LIU).

IEC Mission

Our mission is to engage, serve and support LIU students, faculty, staff, and alumni, as well as students and entrepreneurs throughout the community and region, in the creation of entrepreneurial ventures and innovative business solutions.

IEC Spirit

  • We believe that we were born entrepreneurs, and we encourage everyone to rediscover the entrepreneur in themselves.
  • We believe that in every challenge lies an opportunity, and in every unemployment lies a self-employment venture to service livelihoods and society.
  • We believe in the power of innovation and the encouragement of ambitious mindsets for change.

IEC Vision

Our vision is to build an ecosystem of entrepreneurial minds who can meaningfully and positively impact their communities and be leaders in entrepreneurial ventures for the common good.

Curriculum

  1. Offer an integrated curriculum and experiential learning programs that exceed the expectations of students and businesses.
  2. Accelerate student competencies in leadership and innovation through coaching and training.
